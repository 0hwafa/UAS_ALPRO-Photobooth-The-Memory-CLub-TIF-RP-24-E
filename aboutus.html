The Idea Behind It

The photobooth trend is all the rage among Gen-Z teens these days. It’s not just popping up in physical spaces, but also taking over the digital scene. Driven by the urge to express themselves and instantly share moments, Gen-Z teens inspired the creation of a website called Photobooth: The Memory Club.

With fun features like filters and a variety of photo frames, this website offers a fun and accessible way to capture memories anytime, anywhere — all for free and with friends!

Why This Exists?

This app/website offers a variety of photo frames with eye-catching designs, allowing users to instantly edit their photos right after taking them and no extra apps needed.

The website is open to everyone and completely free to use. It’s easily accessible from any device—whether it’s a phone, tablet, or laptop. Users can take selfies anytime, anywhere, without the need to go anywhere at all.
